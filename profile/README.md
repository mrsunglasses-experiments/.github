# Experiments

</div>

I believe GenAI and LLMs aren’t just tools for generating “slop”.
They can be incredibly powerful for solving real problems, especially for me.

I can use them to solve the problems often of my city and community which often go unnoticed.

Many everyday problems in my city and community feel:

- too small for people to care about,
- too overwhelming to start solving,
- or impossible without huge teams and resources.

I want to challenge that idea.

This organization is a space where I experiment with AI, automation, and open-source technologies to make solving local problems more accessible and achievable.

The goal is simple:

> Identify a problem → build something useful → learn in public → iterate.

---

## Why "Experiments"?

Because not every idea needs to become a startup.

Sometimes:
- a prototype is enough,
- an experiment teaches something valuable,
- or a small tool can genuinely help a few people.

And that matters.

---

## Transparency

Every project will include notes about:
- the idea behind it,
- which AI models and tools were used,
- and how the project was built.

I want the process to stay open, practical, and easy to learn from.
